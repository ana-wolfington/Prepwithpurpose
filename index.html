About the Program

Tutoring that truly matters

Prep with Purpose is a student-led ACT and SAT tutoring program built on a simple idea: students who just crushed the test know exactly what it takes to succeed. Our tutors are high-scorers who've recently sat in the same chair — they know the patterns, the tricks, and the mindset.

We start with a full diagnostic test to pinpoint your strengths and weaknesses, then build focused sessions around exactly what you need. Every session brings you closer to your goal score — and closer to making a real difference for children in Guatemala.

Our Mission

Why I keep going back

Misioneros del Camino has meant the world to me. I've visited multiple times — playing soccer in the field, making bracelets, running around, and just being present with the kids. Each day was full of energy, laughter, and real connection.

These children have so little — and yet so much at the same time. Their immense gratitude and love for everyone around them is something I've never experienced anywhere else. It stays with you.

"The love, joy, and strength of the children I've met there have inspired me to return time and time again — and to do everything I can to support their future."

Leaving was always the hardest part. They cried at goodbyes and chased the bus as it drove away. That image never leaves me. Prep with Purpose exists because of them — and every session brings me one step closer to going back.
